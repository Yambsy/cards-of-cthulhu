About

THE ANCIENT HORROR

The Ancient Ones Have Awakened

"That is not dead which can eternal lie, and with strange aeons even death may die."

In the dark depths of the ocean, something stirs. The ancient city of Cthulhu has risen, and with it, cosmic horrors beyond human comprehension. As an investigator driven by haunting visions, you must navigate a world on the brink of madness.

Uncover eldritch truths as you solve intricate puzzles, navigate treacherous environments, and maintain your sanity while facing beings from beyond the stars. Every decision affects your fate and the world's.

  • Lovecraftian based rogue-like Card Game
  • Atmospheric and theme heavy visuals
  • Card system that affects gameplay

Gameplay

ARCANE MECHANISMS OF FATE

The Eldritch Card System

"The most merciful thing in the world, I think, is the inability of the human mind to correlate all its contents."

In Through the Cards of Cthulhu, destiny is woven through an ancient card system that bridges our world and the cosmic beyond. Each turn, you receive three cards from the Void, each housing powers to harm, heal, or channel arcane magick.

Cards of Affliction

Manifest cosmic horrors to assault your opponent's sanity and physical form.

Cards of Preservation

Invoke ancient rituals that mend wounds and temporarily shield your sanity.

Cards of Eldritch Magick

Command powers beyond mortal comprehension, manipulating reality itself.
