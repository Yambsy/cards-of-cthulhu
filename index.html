About

The Ancient Horror

The Ancient Ones Have Awakened

"That is not dead which can eternal lie, and with strange aeons even death may die."

Inspired by the cosmic horror of H.P. Lovecraft and the strategic depth of games like Slay the Spire and Inscryption, Cards of Cthulhu brings you a unique blend of eldritch horror and tactical card combat.

Wield cards imbued with the power of the Old Ones, each one a fragment of cosmic knowledge that should not exist in our reality. Build your deck, master the forbidden arts, and face horrors beyond mortal comprehension.

  • Lovecraftian based rogue-like Card Game
  • Atmospheric and theme heavy visuals
  • Strategic card-based gameplay
  • Procedurally generated encounters
  • Multiple paths through the nightmare

Features

Arcane Mechanisms of Fate

Cards of Affliction

Manifest cosmic horrors to assault your opponent's sanity. Each card is a window into the void, bringing forth creatures and powers that defy mortal understanding.

Cards of Preservation

Invoke rituals that mend wounds and shield sanity. Ancient knowledge allows you to maintain your grip on reality while delving deeper into the abyss.

Cards of Eldritch Magick

Command powers beyond mortal comprehension. Each spell is a fragment of the Old Ones' knowledge, warping reality to your will.

Diverse Enemies

Face cultists, deep ones, and otherworldly entities, each with unique attack patterns and abilities. Learn their weaknesses or be consumed by the void.

Retro Style

Experience the horror through a lens of retro aesthetics, combining pixel art with modern effects to create an unsettling atmosphere that pays homage to classic horror games.

Built in Unity

Developed using Unity, bringing smooth performance and modern features while maintaining the game's eldritch atmosphere and strategic depth.
